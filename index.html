What is Industrial DevOps?

Bringing Lean, Agile, and DevSecOps to large-scale, safety-critical systems

Industrial DevOps applies the principles that transformed software delivery to complex cyber-physical systems — from submarines to satellites — so teams can build better systems faster, with quality and value built in from the start.

FASTER

Faster delivery

Shorten learning loops and reduce the handoffs and rework that slow complex programs.

SAFER

Quality & safety built in

Engineering rigor, traceability, and governance stay first-class — speed never costs trust.

VALUE

Value to the customer

Success is measured by outcomes delivered, not artifacts produced.
