The Big Idea

AI doesn't just make engineers faster — it changes how teams work.

Traditional product development moves through specialized silos — requirements, systems, architecture, development, test, release — and every handoff adds delay and rework. This workshop shows, live, how GenAI lets one small cross-functional team travel that whole journey in minutes, without losing engineering rigor or human accountability.

SMALLER

Smaller teams

RIGOROUS

Better traceability

A connected, auditable thread from need to release — humans stay accountable throughout.
